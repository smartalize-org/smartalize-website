FAQ

1

Will Smartlitic work with my site?

Smartlitic works out of the box with many popular
CMS, e-commerce platforms, tag managers, CRM
systems, and data-handling tools.


2

Is Smartlitic really free to use
forever?

Yes. You can get started with our free plan which
lets you collect data from up to 2,000 pageviews
per day. If you need to scale the amount of data
you collect, you can always opt for a paid plan.

3

How many users can I add to
my Smartlitic account?

You can add an unlimited amount of users to your
Smartlitic account for free in every plan. Insights
are for sharing! Show your user behavior to
your colleagues and we guarantee they’ll
want their own account.

4

Will Smartlitic slow down my site?

At Smartlitic we take site performance very
seriously and do everything in our power to
optimize everything in the process. Smartlitic's
script, like most scripts and other tracking
solutions, will only have a marginal, almost
negligible, effect on your site's speed and
performance. Smartlitic does not affect your SEO.

5

What are Smartlitic reports?

Automatically collecting data from all pages on
your site can be very expensive and data intensive.
Smartlitic makes it more affordable by taking
an ‘on demand’ report approach.

6

How do I install Smartlitic?

A single tracking code allows you to collect data
from your visitors and send it back to Smartlitic.
Installation involves copy and pasting your
unique Tracking Code within the site you would
like to track.
